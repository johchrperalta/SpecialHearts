ABOUT US

What is Special Hearts?

Special Hearts is a website free and open for children and people young or old. This website is established with the goal of having and molding people escpecially children into something and someone they like through arts! Special Hearts was established to be an advocate for the protection of children's rights in arts, to help meet their basic needs and to expand their opportunities to reach their full potential through arts.


Special Hearts mobilizes political will and material resources to help people, particularly developing children and the youth, ensure a "first call for children" and to build their capacity to form appropriate policies and deliver services for children and their families.


Special Hearts responds in emergencies to protect the rights of children. In coordination with United Nations partners and humanitarian agencies, Special Hearts makes its unique facilities for rapid response available to its partners to relieve the suffering of children and those who provide their care.



Our Vision

Special Hearts envisions a world in which people come together as equals and dialogue so that all children, families and communities have access to the information, skills, technologies, and processes they need to generate solutions;are empowered to make informed choices, reach their full potential; and participate meaningfully in showing their talents and skills through Arts.



Our Mission

The mission of Special Hearts is to collaborate with partners to harness the power of Arts to make a positive difference in the lives of children, their families and communities. Special Hearts promotes the use of a judicious mix of participatory communication and Art strategies and approaches in order to increase the child's development , accelerate achievement of global and development goals and enhance the ability of families and children to achieve results for improvement and realize their rights.
